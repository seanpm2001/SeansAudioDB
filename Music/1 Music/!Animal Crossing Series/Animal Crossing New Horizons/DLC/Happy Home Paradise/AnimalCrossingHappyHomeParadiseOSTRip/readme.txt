Animal Crossing: Happy Home Paradise (どうぶつの森 ハッピーホームパラダイス)

(Officially titled: Animal Crossing: New Horizons - Happy Home Paradise, [あつまれ どうぶつの森 ハッピーホームパラダイス])

----

This is a collection of all music from the Happy Home Paradise DLC for Animal Crossing: New Horizons. The music was ripped from the game's internal audio files and converted to FLAC/MP3, then ordered and tagged.

When new versions of the game come out and add new music tracks, this soundtrack rip will be updated. If anything is wrong or missing, feel free to let me know.

The K.K. tracks are played during the disco party after "finishing" the game. (You can hear it again after that, and the game doesn't actually end.) The track outtros and melodies are used to tie tracks together, based on what key the track is in.
